    Our Projects

    Samagra Groups

    The project, encompassing the BBB Head Office, BBV Corporate Office, and Co-Working Space, exemplified exceptional design prowess. Meticulous attention to detail and a visionary approach resulted in spaces that surpassed expectations.

    Deepa House

    Mrs. Kokila's design for Mrs. Deepa's Mysore home impresses with its thoughtful layout, blending units like Base, Wall, and Loft seamlessly. Innovative additions like Wardrobe with Loft reflect her exceptional creativity and expertise.

    Verdant Garden Abode

    Dr. Shanthala's home features lush gardens leading to the entrance, departing from traditional layouts. Curved architecture enhances the atmosphere, emphasizing the garden-centric experience.

    Ninefold Haven

    Farmhouse tailored for the Fish Land hotel owner, boasting a Hanging Bridge, Amphitheater, Carved & Glass doors, and a Main Building structured on the nine-grid concept, promising a blend of luxury and rural charm in Chikkamagaluru.

    Mkch Choultry

    Crafted for an NRI, this choultry in Hassan embraces a unique design inspired by Islamic architecture, featuring a distinctive Dodecahedron layout. At its heart lies a magnificent 35-foot dome, exemplifying elegance and cultural reverence.

    Flute Serenity

    In the creation of Smt. Smitha's residence, her love for the flute is celebrated through the incorporation of flute holes and peacock feathers. This thoughtful design reflects our dedication and deep involvement in crafting her unique living space.

    Mrs. Kokila K N

    Architect Kokila is renowned for her dedication to proportionate building and her unyielding passion for her craft.
