    Our Projects

    Verdant Garden Abode

    Dr. Shanthala's home features lush gardens leading to the entrance, departing from traditional layouts. Curved architecture enhances the atmosphere, emphasizing the garden-centric experience.

    Ninefold Haven

    Farmhouse tailored for the Fish Land hotel owner, boasting a Hanging Bridge, Amphitheater, Carved & Glass doors, and a Main Building structured on the nine-grid concept, promising a blend of luxury and rural charm.

    Mkch Choultry

    Crafted for an NRI, this choultry in Hassan embraces a unique design inspired by Islamic architecture, featuring a distinctive Dodecahedron layout. At its heart lies a magnificent 35-foot dome, exemplifying elegance and cultural reverence.
